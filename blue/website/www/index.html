Brutalismbot

A Web More Brutalist

Brutalismbot is a mechanical marvel that mirrors posts from /r/brutalism to other platforms.

Every 15 minutes, fresh content from /r/brutalism/new is plucked and published to Brutalismbot's clients.

Slack

Choose a #channel on your Slack workspace and Brutalismbot will push new posts to your workspace as they are discovered.

Twitter

Follow Brutalismbot on Twitter to see, like, and retweet brutalism on your timeline.

Support

Problems with Brutalismbot? Send me an email at help@brutalismbot.com

For faster results, you can always file an issue or view the source code on GitHub.

Privacy Policy
